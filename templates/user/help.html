Help

Searching for News

To search for news, use the search bar located at the top-right corner of the website. Type your query and press enter or click the magnifying glass icon. You can search by keywords, titles, or authors.

Navigating Categories

You can find different news categories in the navigation bar at the top of the page. Click on any category to view the news related to that category. Examples of categories include Politics, Sports, Entertainment, and Technology.

Creating an Account

To get a more personalized experience, you can create an account on NewsPortal. By creating an account, you can save articles to read later, comment on articles, and receive customized news recommendations.

  1. Click on 'Login' or 'Sign Up' usually located at the top right of the page.
  2. Fill in the registration form with your email address, a password, and any other required information.
  3. Login with your new credentials and start exploring!
